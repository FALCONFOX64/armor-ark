## About Section (Copy-Paste Ready for LinkedIn)

Armor Ark provides fractional Chief Information Security Officer (CISO) leadership and responsible AI programs to organizations navigating the rapid adoption of advanced AI systems.

We embed security, ethics, and governance into AI initiatives — delivering strategic security leadership, risk management, compliance frameworks, and ethical AI design without the overhead of a full-time executive. Our engagements give technical teams the freedom to move fast while providing executives and boards the assurance, transparency, and accountability they demand.

Key service areas include:
- Fractional CISO Services — risk assessments, security program development, board reporting, incident readiness, and AI-specific security strategy.
- Responsible AI Governance — ethical frameworks, bias audits, transparency mechanisms, and alignment with the EU AI Act, NIST AI RMF, and emerging global standards.
- AI Security & Risk Management — adversarial testing, prompt injection defenses, model supply chain security, and continuous monitoring of AI threats.
- AI Compliance & Assurance — audit-ready documentation, governance structures, third-party assessments, and demonstrable accountability.

Every client dashboard, governance portal, risk visualization interface, and reporting experience we deliver is designed and built by the **Armor Ark Web Team** — specialists in high-performance visual systems, intuitive information architecture, and cross-audience experience design. The Armor Ark website itself is a living demonstration of these capabilities.

We believe responsible innovation requires both world-class security leadership and exceptional execution. Armor Ark helps ambitious organizations build AI that is secure, ethical, and trustworthy.
